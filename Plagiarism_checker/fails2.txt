In todays economy, a currency is a medium of exchange that allows you to make transactions. 
In the history, the currency has had different forms, such as gold, silver and other forms.
 Today's currency is much more regulated because it is physically made in the form of paper banknotes or coins.
 The value of almost all of the currencies is guaranteed by government promises. Although physical currencies (coins and banknotes)
 are used on a daily basis, the biggest currency exchange transactions are conducted automatically.
 Modern currency exchange rate system technology for exchanging currency between digital accounts.
